Room History Rules

These rules govern the Marathon Server squad auction chamber. All operators are expected to uphold competitive integrity and host authority at all times.

Auction mechanics

Auctions run in discrete lots (rounds). Only the Head Admin may open a lot or finalize it. When a lot is live, all four squad leaders may place bids or skip. Skipping records your squad as out for that lot but does not end bidding for other squads.

Valid bidding

  • Bids must be whole integers.
  • Each bid must be greater than or equal to the published minimum for the lot.
  • Each bid must be strictly greater than the current highest valid bid.
  • No bid may exceed your squad’s current treasury balance at the moment it is validated.
  • After your squad skips the current lot, you cannot bid on that lot.

Invalid bids are rejected immediately by the system. Attempts may be logged in the live telemetry feed for transparency and review.

Treasury rules

Each squad maintains a persistent treasury balance stored server-side. Balances are only reduced when a lot is finalized and your squad is the clear winner with the highest valid bid. Opening bids or leading during a round does not debit the treasury until finalize.

Transfers & history

When a lot is sold, the winning amount is deducted from the winner’s treasury, the lot is recorded in auction history, and ledger entries are written for audit. If no valid bids exist at finalize, the lot is marked UNSOLD and no treasury movement occurs.

Admin authority

The Head Admin controls the tempo of the event: creating lots, publishing minimums and descriptions, and issuing the sole Finalize action that closes a lot and locks results. Admin decisions within this system are final for the purposes of the auction session.

Conduct & penalties

Collusion, account sharing, attempting to bypass validation, or interfering with other operators’ sessions is prohibited. Off-platform penalties (strikes, disqualification from future auctions) are at Marathon Server staff discretion and are not limited to what this software can detect.
